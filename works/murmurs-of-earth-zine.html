MURMURS OF EARTH ZINE

What will you send to the universe to describe yourself?
I interviewed some people about this question and designed this zine. I got some different answers, such as an image of letters, a photo of food, some cash, a screenshot of Instagram, shoes, the vinyl of the Beatles, etc.
I also got the answer from Jon Lomberg, the designer of the Golden Record project, he would like to send a photo of Galaxy Garden he made in Hawai.
