10 CHINESE WORDS

These illustrations are commissioned by the Culture Trip in response to the article ‘ 10 Chinese Words You Can't Translate Into English’.
I drew a night view of a famous street in China for the header illustration, which can show a modern and lively China. For the body illustrations, I focused on the words 緣分 – yuán fèn and 香 – xiāng. Check the article at the Culture Trip
